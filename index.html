Ever since I was a young boy I have had a keen interest in gaming. I began playing video games around the age of six while I lived in Mexico. I vividly remember the distinct start up noise of the Playstation 2 my friends had and the countless crazy memories we made together on GTA San Andreas. Wether it be on family's old computer, a mobile phone, or whatever console I had managed to get my hands on video games played a large role in my childhood. Video games have given me so many good memories over the years and so I want to spread that joy to as many others as possible.

We sell all variety of video games and video game related items. We have all sorts of consoles from old to new. We also sell PC's, monitors, gaming headphones, chairs, mouse & keyboards, and controllers. We provide all of these products at an affordable price unlike most other stores which are similar to this one (Gamestop, BestBuy, etc). We also offer a repair service in which customers can bring in any malfunctioning equipment and have it repaired free of cost (if a warranty was purchased). If we do not have a particular item available when you visit then we can order it and ship it to your house.
