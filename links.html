Steam Homepage

Steam is an online video game store which provides games for PC and Mac users. Publishers can upload there game to the platform and consumers will review their games. Steam also has seperate communities for each game where it can be discussed by the players. It was created by the Valve Corporation and first launched in 2003. Steam is by far the largest video game platform for people who play on their computers.
Gamestop Homepage

GameStop is a partner company to us and provides some of the same products and services that we do. They offer video games of all types, hardware for various consoles (such as headphones, controllers, chargers, the consoles themselves, etc.), and a trade-in service where you can earn money by turning in some of your old games or consoles. They were founded in August of 2001 and was formerly known as Babbage's which was founded in 1984 in Dallas, Texas.
LevelUpVideoGames Homepage

LevelUpVideoGames is a company thats products range from retro to modern. They sell equipment, merch, consoles, and video games for even the most niche of gaming systems. Their company is called Retro Paradise and was founded reletively recently in 2016. They are the smallest of the companies listed here but that does not mean that they are any less reliable.
